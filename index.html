About Us

In the late 2010s, a group of talented and ambitious software engineers and entrepreneurs decided to create a company that would revolutionize the way we communicate and share information. This company, known as ConnectIT, started in a small garage in Cluj-Napoca, Romania.

After years of hard work, countless lines of code, and relentless dedication, we launched our first product in 2020. The company's commitment to user privacy and data security set it apart from the competition, earning the trust of millions of users.

As the years passed, ConnectIT continued to innovate. We expanded our platform to include features like cloud storage, virtual meetings, and collaboration tools.

In its early years, ConnectIT focused on providing comprehensive IT consulting services, helping businesses identify and implement tailored solutions to enhance efficiency and productivity. The company quickly gained a reputation for its customer-centric approach, technical expertise, and commitment to delivering results.

Projects

VirtuMeet

VirtuMeet is a groundbreaking virtual meeting and collaboration platform that transcends the limitations of traditional video conferencing. It combines augmented and virtual reality technologies to create a highly immersive and productive meeting experience. With VirtuMeet, users can have lifelike interactions, share 3D models, and collaborate in a shared virtual space, no matter where they are in the world.

EcoSaver

EcoSaver is a sustainable technology solution designed to reduce energy consumption and carbon emissions in office environments. It combines IoT sensors, AI, and smart building management to optimize lighting, heating, and cooling systems. EcoSaver provides real-time data and predictive analytics to help companies reduce their carbon footprint while saving on energy costs. Sustainability practices reduce environmental impact.

CipherGuard

CipherGuard is a state-of-the-art cybersecurity platform that provides end-to-end protection for businesses. It offers advanced threat detection, data encryption, conversation encryption and multi-factor authentication to safeguard sensitive information. CipherGuard's machine learning algorithms continuously adapt to evolving cyber threats, ensuring the utmost security for organizations.
